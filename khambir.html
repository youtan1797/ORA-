🍞 Khambir

Ladakh’s sacred sourdough — hearty, round, and full of story.

Baked with high-altitude wisdom, Khambir is more than bread — it's a celebration of community, survival, and soul.

Traditionally eaten with butter tea or chutney, its thick crust and fermented base reflect the resilience of Ladakhi life.

What Makes It Special

  • 🌾 Local barley and wheat blend
  • 🔥 Baked over stone or clay ovens
  • 💨 Naturally fermented
  • 🫱🏼‍🫲🏽 Shared across generations
